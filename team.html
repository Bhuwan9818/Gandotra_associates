Founder & Managing Principal

Raghav Gandotra

Mr. Gandotra is the Founder and Managing Principal of the Firm. He was admitted to the Bar in 2006. With over a decade and a half of experience in Civil, Matrimonial, Family, Property, and Criminal litigation, he has advised numerous domestic and international clients on Corporate-Commercial Transactions & Advisory, Project Finance, M&A, and Arbitration matters.

He regularly appears before the Supreme Court of India and the High Courts of Delhi, Kolkata, Bombay, Chandigarh, Bangalore, and Nainital. Before founding the firm, he was associated with the chambers of a Senior Advocate, where he practiced extensively in the Supreme Court.
