NRI Help Desk

We at Gandotra & Associates assist non-resident Indians, foreign citizens, and persons of Indian origin to manage their legal affairs and resolve legal issues within and outside India.

Our Main Motive

NRIs and other individuals residing in foreign countries looking for legal services often find themselves in the middle of a complex web of legal situations — sometimes affecting their overseas immigration status, career, or even their ability to visit India.

Unlike Indian residents, legal cases involving NRIs may engage multiple areas of law and require dealing with Indian authorities. Many NRIs struggle to obtain even basic information about the Indian legal system, applicable laws, or lawyers. We focus exclusively on serving NRIs and foreign nationals across Family, Divorce, Criminal, Property, Civil, Business, Real Estate, Immigration, and Tax law.

Assuring You Peace of Mind

Our fees are competitive and vary case by case. You will not need to make perpetual visits to India to appear in Court in person — saving you loss of earnings while away from home, as well as personal safety in serious cases.

In property matters, clients often worry they don't possess documents related to the property and hesitate to make a claim. Our team — without requiring your presence in India — obtains all property-related documents, establishing the chain of title and strategically concluding whether a claim can be successfully made.

Courts We Appear Before

Our lawyers appear before the Hon'ble Supreme Court of India, the High Courts of Delhi, Punjab & Haryana, Jammu & Kashmir, Allahabad, and Bombay, along with all lower courts of Delhi, Punjab, Haryana, Pune, Bangalore, Jammu, Mumbai, and Kolkata — as well as District Consumer Courts, NCLT, and the National Consumer Forum.
