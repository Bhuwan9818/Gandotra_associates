Criminal Defence

Criminal Lawyer

Facing criminal charges is one of the most stressful experiences in life — your liberty, reputation, and future may all be at stake. Our criminal defence team provides aggressive, strategic representation from the very first FIR through trial and appeal.

Our Criminal Defence Practice

We represent clients in cases related to cheating, fraud, and a wide spectrum of criminal law matters, working tirelessly to secure favourable decisions from the Court. Our team regularly appears before Sessions Courts, the High Court of Delhi, and the Supreme Court of India.

Why Speed Matters

In criminal matters, the first 24–48 hours after an FIR is registered are often the most critical. Early legal intervention — securing anticipatory bail, ensuring procedural compliance, and protecting your rights during investigation — can materially change the trajectory of a case. Our team is available to act swiftly when time is of the essence.

Our Approach

We combine rigorous case analysis with assertive courtroom advocacy. Every defence strategy is built around the specific facts of your case, applicable precedent, and a clear understanding of how investigating agencies and courts are likely to approach the matter.
