Property Services for NRIs

NRI Property Management

Managing property in India from abroad presents unique challenges — from absentee ownership risks to fraudulent transfers. We provide end-to-end property management and protection services exclusively designed for NRIs.

Why NRIs Need Specialised Help

Properties owned by NRIs are often vulnerable to illegal occupation, fraudulent sale by relatives or third parties, and disputed inheritance claims — precisely because the owner is unable to monitor the property regularly. We act as your eyes, ears, and legal hand on the ground in India.

Our Property Management Services

✓

Title verification and chain-of-title document search

✓

Recovery of property from illegal occupants or tenants

✓

Transfer and mutation of ownership rights

✓

Partition and separation of share in jointly-owned property

✓

Power of Attorney drafting and registration for remote management

✓

Sale, purchase, and lease assistance on your behalf

✓

Property tax compliance and municipal record updates

How We Work Remotely

We use secure digital communication and notarised Power of Attorney instruments to act entirely on your behalf, ensuring every step — from document retrieval to court representation — proceeds without requiring your physical presence in India.
